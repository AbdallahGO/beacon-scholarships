**Application preparation — paid ("the ticket").** For a fee shown before you pay, our team prepares your application for one specific scholarship: a professional CV built for that program, an essay developed together with you, a review and organization of every required document into a complete application package, and preparation for the scholarship's exam or interview if it has one.
