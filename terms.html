For a fee shown before you pay, our team prepares your application for one specific scholarship: a professional CV built for that program, an essay developed together with you, a review and organization of every required document into a complete application package, and preparation for the scholarship's exam or interview if it has one.

Timing. Work begins as soon as your booking is confirmed, and your complete application package is delivered within three days — unless a specific scholarship requires longer, in which case we tell you the honest timeline before you pay.

Your part. The three days work when you do your part: provide truthful information and authentic documents, answer our questions promptly, and be available for the essay conversation. Delays caused by missing documents or slow responses extend the timeline by the same amount.

Authenticity. Everything we produce is built from your real story and your real records. We will not fabricate achievements, invent experiences, or work with forged documents — and if we discover falsified documents or information, we stop work immediately without refund. The essay is developed with you and remains your work; you are its author, and you are responsible for reviewing your scholarship provider's rules about outside assistance before submitting.

Submission. We prepare the package and guide you through submitting it, but the application is yours: final submission, meeting the provider's deadline, and attending any exam or interview are your responsibility. We'll prepare you for them — we can't attend them for you.
