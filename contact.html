Get in touch

We’re a small team building Beacon for students like you. Whether you have a question before booking, something’s broken or missing, or you just want to say hello — we read every message.

Send us a message

This opens Gmail in a new tab with your message ready to send.

What can we help with?

  • Before you book — anything you want to understand about a ticket, the fee, or the exam and interview steps before you pay.
  • Your booking — a question about a ticket you’ve booked, your cooldown, your ticket code, or buying extra ticket space.
  • Your account — trouble signing in, your profile details, or resetting your password.
  • Report a listing — a scholarship that’s closed, wrong, or has a broken link.
  • Feedback, ideas & partnerships — anything that would make Beacon more useful; universities and organisations, we’d love to talk.

Prefer email? Reach us directly at Beacon_Team_Help@gmail.com

We usually reply within a couple of days. For the common questions — what a ticket costs, what it includes, and what happens next — the FAQ may answer it faster.
